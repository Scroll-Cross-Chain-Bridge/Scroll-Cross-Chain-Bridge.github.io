What is Scroll Cross Chain Bridge?

Scroll is a zkEVM Layer 2 network for Ethereum, and the official portal provides the guided route for moving supported assets between Ethereum and Scroll. Review the official bridge docs before signing.

How Scroll Cross Chain Bridge works

  1. Open the portalGo to the official Scroll bridge page and connect the wallet you want to use.
  2. Choose the routeSelect Ethereum or Scroll as the source network, pick a supported asset, and enter the amount.
  3. Review the transactionCheck the destination network, token, amount, and gas details before confirming in your wallet.
  4. Track settlementUse the portal status and explorer links to follow the transfer; withdrawals back to Ethereum may require finalization.

Before you bridge

Official routing

Start from Scroll's official site or the portal URL, then verify the domain before connecting a wallet.

Network clarity

The flow separates Ethereum and Scroll so users can confirm direction, asset, and destination before signing.

Explorer checks

After signing, use the portal status and Scrollscan to verify activity on Scroll.

Scroll Cross Chain Bridge FAQ

What is the scroll cross chain bridge?

It is the user route to Scroll's official portal for moving supported assets between Ethereum and Scroll with a connected wallet.

Is scroll cross chain bridge the official Scroll app?

This page is not the official app; use the action button to open portal.scroll.io/bridge and verify the domain.

Which networks does scroll cross chain bridge focus on?

The primary route is between Ethereum mainnet and Scroll, Scroll's Ethereum Layer 2 network.

Do I need a wallet for scroll cross chain bridge?

Yes. Bridging requires a compatible wallet, enough assets for the transfer, and ETH for network gas where applicable.

Are scroll cross chain bridge fees fixed?

No fixed fee claim is made here. Review live gas costs, token details, and any portal-displayed charges before signing.
